HeatHarvester

An Idea by Lorenzo De Angelis to Save the World

Lorenzo De Angelis, born in Rome on December 9, 1994, has created and donated this technology to the world, not for profit or fame, but for the well-being of humanity and the planet.

HeatHarvester is an open-source project that aims to convert ambient heat — the unwanted byproduct of climate change — into usable clean electricity. This technology is offered freely as a hopeful and practical contribution to our future.
